## SECTION 4: FAQ COPY

**Q: How long does it take to deploy a 3.0 system?**
A: Most systems go live in 2-4 weeks from discovery to launch. It depends on complexity and data availability. We work fast because we use templates, not custom engineering.

---

**Q: What if my business has unique needs?**
A: That's actually the point. We don't try to fit you into a mold. During discovery, we map your workflows, revenue model, team structure. Then we architect a 3.0 system that fits YOU. Yes, it takes more design upfront, but it saves you months of customization and frustration.

---

**Q: How does the ownership model work?**
A: You own 51-70% of your 3.0 system (negotiable). The rest can be distributed to team members, investors, or partners. Revenue (from AI services, digital products, reseller fees) flows to your wallet based on your % ownership. Blockchain makes it transparent and automatic.

---

**Q: Can I really onboard my own clients into my 3.0 system?**
A: Yes. Your 3.0 system is multi-tenant. You can spin up sub-instances for clients. They get isolated environments, their own dashboards, their own AI agents. They pay you monthly SaaS or transaction fees. You keep 70-80% of that revenue.

---

**Q: What if WhatsApp goes down or changes their API?**
A: Your 3.0 system has fallback mechanisms built in. If WhatsApp fails, it can switch to SMS, email, or in-app notifications. We monitor integrations and proactively test failovers. Your business doesn't stop because one channel hiccups.

---

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use encryption at rest and in transit. Row-level security at the database layer means each tenant's data is isolated. We maintain audit logs. Compliance frameworks (GDPR, data residency, etc.) are configurable. Full details in our security docs.

---

**Q: What if I don't believe in blockchain/tokenization?**
A: That's fine. The blockchain layer is optional. You can use a traditional ownership agreement instead. But we recommend it because it makes revenue sharing transparent and automatic—no manual accounting, no disputes about who gets paid what.

---

**Q: What does Alphadome make money from?**
A: We share in your success. We take a % of transaction fees (typically 3-8%, depending on volume), a small monthly infrastructure fee (~$100-500 depending on system size), and upside on digital product sales. You own the majority stake. We're aligned.

---

**Q: Can I migrate away from Alphadome later?**
A: Yes. Your 3.0 system is yours. All code, data, credentials, and ownership records are yours. If you want to migrate to another provider or run it yourself, you can. We believe you won't want to because the Alphadome infrastructure keeps improving, but the option exists.

## SECTION 9: MESSAGING GUARDRAILS

### Use Often:
- "3.0 system" (not "tool," "platform," "bot")
- "You own it" (emphasizes control)
- "Co-build" (emphasizes partnership)
- "Tokenized" (modern, precise)
- "Revenue share" (shows alignment)
- "AI agents" (better than "chatbot")
- "Multiply" (better than "automate" — less threatening)
- "WhatsApp native" (they already live there)

### Avoid:
- "Chatbot" (cheap, generic, consumer-facing)
- "Automation" (sounds like job replacement)
- "SaaS" (boring, suggests renting)
- "Complicated" (use "thorough" or "custom" instead)
- "Cheap" (use "efficient" or "affordable")
- Vague promises without mechanics
- Comparing to competitors (stay positive)
