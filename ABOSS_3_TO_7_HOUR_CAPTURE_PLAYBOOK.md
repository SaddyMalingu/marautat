# A Boss 3-7 Hour Market Capture Playbook

Goal: Generate revenue within the current window using non-nagging, multi-channel outreach.

## Hour 0-1: Build the Contact Stack

1. Use queries in `ABOSS_RAPID_CONTACT_RESEARCH.md`.
2. Capture 60 contacts in a sheet using the CSV template columns.
3. Rank top 20 by `priority_score` for immediate outreach.

## Hour 1-2: Multi-Channel First Touch (Value-Led)

### Email Script

Subject: Quick growth idea for {{organization}}

Hi {{name}},

Noticed {{organization}} is active in {{industry}}. We help teams reduce lead response delays and close more opportunities using lightweight automation.

If useful, I can share a 2-step setup plan tailored to your workflow.

Reply `YES` and I will send it.

Regards,
{{your_name}}

### LinkedIn / DM Script

Hi {{name}},
I work with teams in {{industry}} to tighten follow-up and conversion flow without adding extra headcount.
If you want, I can send a short 2-step plan specific to {{organization}}.

### Phone Opener

Hi {{name}}, quick one. I support businesses like yours to improve lead-to-sale conversion. If I share one practical workflow that can be implemented today, would that be useful?

## Hour 2-4: Follow-up Only for Engaged Contacts

Follow-up rule: Do not follow up cold no-response contacts yet.

### Engaged Follow-up Script

Thanks for the response.
Based on your setup, I recommend:
1) response-time automation,
2) payment recovery workflow.

If you want, we can start with a same-day pilot and measure impact this week.

## Hour 4-6: Conversion Push

1. Call top 10 engaged contacts.
2. Offer a same-day pilot slot.
3. Ask for explicit commitment: `Shall we start today at {{time}}?`

## Hour 6-7: Close + Debrief

1. Send final same-day slot reminder to engaged contacts only.
2. Record:
   - contacts reached
   - positive replies
   - calls completed
   - pilot commitments
   - paid conversions

## Non-Nagging Policy

1. Max 2 touches per contact in this window unless they engage.
2. Value first, CTA second.
3. No repeated urgency without context.
