Welcome to Tenant Onboarding

  1. Welcome & Introduction

    Get started with a guided onboarding process. Estimated time: 10-20 minutes.

  2. Business Information

    Provide your business name, contact details, and admin info.
    Why? This helps us personalize your experience and support.

  3. WhatsApp Business API Setup

    • Register a WhatsApp Business Account.
    • Create a Facebook Business Manager account.
    • Complete business verification.
    • Generate WhatsApp API credentials (App ID, Secret, Phone Number ID, etc.).

    Checklist: App ID, App Secret, Phone Number ID, WABA ID, Access Token.

  4. Bot Configuration

    Enter your WhatsApp API credentials in the portal.
    Use the Test Connection button to verify integration.

  5. Feature Enablement

    Choose which features to enable: Catalog, Campaigns, Dashboard, etc.

  6. Media & Catalog Upload

    Upload your product catalog and media. Download CSV Template.

  7. Testing & Go-Live

    Review your setup, send a test message, and confirm you’re ready to go live.

  8. Support & Resources

    Need help? Contact Support or view the Setup Guide.
